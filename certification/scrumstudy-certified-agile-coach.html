Overview

SCRUMstudy™ Certified Agile Coach (SCAC™) prepares Scrum practitioners to manage complex Scrum projects and implement Scrum in complex projects involving big project teams, programs and portfolios. It also helps practitioners to get multiple implementation and consultation projects in Scrum.

Audience Profile:

SCRUMstudy™ Certified Agile Coach (SCAC™) certification is meant for those SCRUM consultants/training partners, whose primary business is to provide consultancy and help in implementing Scrum in other organizations

Prerequisites

To become a SCRUMstudy™ Certified Agile Coach you should fulfil the following criteria:

  • Should have more than 3 years of experience managing Scrum/Agile Projects and more than 6 years of project management experience.
  • Should be SCRUMstudy™ Certified Trainer (SCT®).

How to become a SCRUMstudy™ Certified Agile Coach

  • Submit the application and provide details of the Scrum/Agile projects implemented.
  • Pay $500 towards SCRUMstudy™ SCAC™ application fee.

After reviewing the documents submitted by the applicant, SCRUMstudy™ certifies the applicant as SCRUMstudy™ Certified Agile Coach (SCAC™) - and they will be awarded an accreditation certificate making them eligible to provide consultancy and help in implementing Scrum in other organizations.
