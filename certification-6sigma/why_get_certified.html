The world is increasingly adopting Six Sigma. Are you?

6sigmastudy™ certifications are widely accepted and help individuals and organizations complete Six Sigma projects with greater efficiency, increased ROI, and higher level of customer satisfaction.

Home Certification Why get certified
Many organizations around the globe adopted Six Sigma and Lean to increase quality of their outputs and to reduce cost by means of reducing defects and removing inefficiencies. There are numerous benefits of adopting Six Sigma.  

For Individuals

Growing popularity and acceptability of Six Sigma and Lean have created a greater demand for certified professionals in the job market. Grab the opportunity and join this growing community.

For Organizations

Six Sigma and Lean certified professionals help organizations with improved level of quality and a dramatic reduction in defects leading to increased ROI.

For A.T.P.

6sigmastudy™ helps training partners to tap into the market potential of Six Sigma certifications quickly and without much upfront cost.
