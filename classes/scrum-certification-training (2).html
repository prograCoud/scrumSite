2,100+ SCRUMstudy™ Authorized Training Partners across 50+ countries conduct physical and virtual instructor-led classes for advanced certifications such as Scrum Master Certified (SMC®), Scrum Product Owner Certified (SPOC®), and SCRUMstudy Agile Master Certified (SAMC™).

SCRUMstudy™ also conducts free 5-hour Scrum Webinar and Virtual Instructor-led Training for Scrum Fundamentals Certified (SFC™) certification exam.

Employees of 7500+ companies from 150 countries have enrolled with SCRUMstudy™/VMEdu®. More

All certifications are based on ‘A Guide to the Scrum Body of Knowledge (SBOK® Guide)’. Download free e-copy of SBOK® Guide.
